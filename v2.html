Formula 1 - Australian GP Winners Performance Dashboard

Overview

This dashboard explores how Australian Grand Prix winners performed across each Formula 1 season. It connects their race-day success in Melbourne to broader patterns in driver dominance, team competitiveness, and performance efficiency.

Performance Against Teammates

Compares how Australian GP winners performed against their teammates across each season. In all seasons except for Bottas in 2019 and Sainz in 2024, Australian GP winners have outperformed their teammates across the season. Verstappen's performance in the 2023 season is notable, winning a record 19 races in a season.

Australian GP Winners vs Constructor Performance

Again, Australian GP–winning drivers’ have matched the respective performance of their team in driver/constructor championships in all years except 2019 and 2024. Whilst Bottas was unable to beat a dominant Hamilton for the championship, Sainz lost out to both his teammate, Verstappen and the two McLaren drivers despite having the 2nd/3rd fastest car.

Qualifying vs Finishing Efficiency

Visualises how well drivers converted qualifying performance into race-day finishes. Top-left = consistently strong qualifying and race results. Verstappen in 2023 is the clear outlier, greatly outperforming his qualifying results on Sunday.
