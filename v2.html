Formula 1 Performance Dashboard

Overview

This dashboard explores how Australian Grand Prix winners performed across each Formula 1 season. It connects their race-day success in Melbourne to broader patterns in driver dominance, team competitiveness, and performance efficiency.

Performance Against Teammates

Compares how Australian GP winners performed against their teammates across each season, revealing intra-team performance gaps and consistency trends.

Driver vs Constructor Performance (2018–2024)

Shows how Australian GP–winning drivers’ final standings compared with their constructors’ championship positions.

Qualifying vs Finishing Efficiency

Visualises how well drivers converted qualifying performance into race-day finishes. Top-left = consistently strong qualifying and race results.
